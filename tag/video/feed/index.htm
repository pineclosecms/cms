Details:
On September 28, 2024, at approximately 11:05 AM, officers arrived at Pine Close in an attempt to conduct a Vulnerability Assessment (VA) for the resident of flat no#14. This was not their first attempt, as the resident has a history of ignoring both the police and WDH.
The officers departed at precisely 11:09 AM. Less than one minute later, the resident was observed exiting his flat with his dog.
Upon noticing a police vehicle driving down a street out of camera view, he was seen hastily returning to his flat, pulling the dog along.
At 3:49 PM, nearly five hours after the initial attempt, the police returned to make another effort to communicate with the resident of flat no#14.
This time, they waited longer than before and the officers became aware that the resident was present and deliberately ignoring them, as he was spotted in the window and overheard shouting at his dog for barking.
At 3:52 PM, after being ignored once again, the police left the premises.
Subsequently, the resident remained inside the apartment for approximately six hours, during which time intermittent stomping, banging, and shouting were heard.
from 9:30 PM, the resident of flat no#14 was observed walking around outside our flat on multiple occasions over several hours.
At 10:32 PM, he can be seen looking through our window while passing our flat.
Following this, he returned to his apartment briefly, where stomping noises were heard for a couple of minutes. He then came back outside and continued to walk around.
